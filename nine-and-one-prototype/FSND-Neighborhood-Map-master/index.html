Nine and One

  • A quick way to learn about a small part of Northern California

Welcome to Nine and One. I'm an educational app that will tell you a little bit about the bay area, its regions, and counties + Santa Cruz (not technically the bay area). Select from the drop down list to see the counties that are considered to be within a region. Next, click on a county to see the weather conditions of that county, and a photo of a landmark in that county. That landmark sits at the highlighted marker. Click the marker to get a little information, info courtesy of the Wikipedia API.
